About Stackly

Our Story

From Startup to
Global IT Partner

Stackly was born in a modest office in Bangalore with a simple ambition: make enterprise technology accessible to every business. Twelve years later, we serve clients across 28 countries with a team of over 800 engineers, architects and consultants.

We believe technology is a means, not an end. Our measure of success is the business outcomes our clients achieve — revenue growth, operational efficiency, customer satisfaction.

Every engagement begins with deep listening and ends only when the client's goals are met. That philosophy has earned us a 97% client retention rate.
